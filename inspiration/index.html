Artists That Inspire Me

by Gabriel Roca


Tim Burton

Known for his distinctive style that blends gothic and whimsical elements, Tim Burton successfully creates a dream fantasy world that stays true to his artistic vision.
His ability to capture and master such dark yet beautiful visuals in a way that is easily recognizable is a testament to his unique, rare talent.
I would love to get to a point in my life and career where I can continously create art that is true to me especially having the freedom to express that in different mediums.

About Tim Burton

Paramore

The American rock band, Paramore is composed of the talented Hayley Williams, Taylor York, and Zac Farro.
Paramore has been my favoriye band of all time because their music brings out all the emotions: angst, misery, joy, and especially the feeling of being alive.
Their lyrics, sound, and who they are as gtenuine people inspire me to be the best version of myself in my passions.
Altogether, they remind me how vulnerbility and goodness only makes us stronger and capable.

Keith Haring

Popular artist and activist, Keith Haring, was part of a legendary New York art scene during the 1980s.
Growing up, I saw his artwork quite frequently and was automatically drawn to his colorful, bold, and playful appearance.
As I got older, I became evem more appreciative of his work because of how they responded to contemporary social and political events.
I am moved by his creations because of it is powerful purpose alongside its striking visuals.
Keith Haring inspires me to beep creating art especially to paint a message that is important to me and what I believe in.
